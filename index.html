Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about JustLend DeFi

What is JustLend DeFi?

JustLend DeFi is a decentralized financial platform in the TRON ecosystem that allows users to earn interest on their crypto assets, borrow against collateral, and swap tokens with minimal fees.

What is the staking APY?

The current staking APY reaches up to 120% depending on the selected pool and lock-up period. APY may vary depending on market conditions.

Is the platform secure?

JustLend DeFi uses advanced security technologies, including audits from leading companies and multi-signature smart contracts. The platform runs on the TRON blockchain, known for its high performance and security.

How to connect a wallet?

To connect a wallet, click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top right corner and select your preferred TRON wallet (TronLink, Trust Wallet, and others). Confirm the connection in your wallet.
